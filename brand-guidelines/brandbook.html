Reduced motion Observed
A global prefers-reduced-motion query collapses every animation and transition to 0.01ms — page-enter, flash-saved, the rail slide, bar-expand and drawers all neutralise. Motion is decorative, never load-bearing.

Findings — reconcile Recommended
1. --text-* & --radius-* vars in globals.css (30/24/19; 6/8/12) are unused — Tailwind's scale (40/28/22; 6/10/14) renders. Delete or sync the vars.
2. .brand-mark-gradient comment says "chartreuse" — the gradient is violet. Stale from a prior pass.
3. Foundation --accent = n-900; the Soft Violet block overrides it. Comment the block out for a grayscale fallback.
